ABOUT LOOKSM

"Lookism" is a popular South Korean webtoon (manhwa) written and illustrated by Park Tae-jun. It explores themes of appearance, identity, and social hierarchy, primarily focusing on how societal perceptions of physical attractiveness influence people's lives.

SYSPNOSIS

"Lookism" follows the life of a high school student named Park Hyung-suk (also known as Daniel Park in some translations). Hyung-suk is overweight, unattractive, and frequently bullied by his peers because of his appearance. His life takes a dramatic turn when he mysteriously gains the ability to switch between two bodies: his original, overweight body and a new, handsome and physically fit body.

THEMES

Duality of Appearance: Hyung-suk's experience with two different bodies allows him to see firsthand the stark contrast in how people treat him based on his looks. In his handsome body, he is treated with respect and admiration, while in his original body, he faces prejudice and bullying.

Bullying and Social Issues: The manhwa addresses the serious issue of bullying in schools and how it affects victims. It also explores other social issues like discrimination, self-esteem, and the pressure to conform to societal beauty standards.

Personal Growth: Throughout the story, Hyung-suk learns valuable lessons about self-worth, empathy, and the importance of inner beauty. His experiences in both bodies help him grow as a person and understand the superficiality of judging others based on appearance.

Complex Characters: "Lookism" features a wide range of characters, each with their own backstories and struggles. These characters include friends, enemies, and love interests, all contributing to the rich narrative and Hyung-suk's journey.

Humor and Satire: The manhwa balances its serious themes with humor and satire, often poking fun at societal norms and the absurdity of judging people solely based on looks.

IMPACT AND PAPULARITY

Lookism" has garnered a significant following both in South Korea and internationally. Its relatable themes, engaging storylines, and thought-provoking commentary on appearance and identity have resonated with readers. The webtoon has been praised for its unique premise and its ability to address important social issues in an accessible and entertaining way.

ADAPTATION

Due to its popularity, "Lookism" has also inspired adaptations, including a live-action drama and an animated series, further expanding its reach and impact. Overall, "Lookism" is a compelling manhwa that delves into the complexities of appearance and identity, offering readers a blend of drama, humor, and social commentary.
